Playtest Notes - Ayoka, Nicole, Zada - 3/31/26

Version: v1.0.2
Platform: Windows (PC)

A. Misc.
- Music is nice.

B. Dialogue
- Nicole and Zada were a bit confused with how to continue the dialogue.
	- Nicole immediately figured she was supposed to use the spacebar.
	- Zada tried to click the small white arrow at the bottom but found it difficult to click and continue.
- "I think we're Skye, and that's Judith." -- Nicole and Zada were a bit confused as to who each of the characters were in the scene.
	- Suggestion: overhead tags or something to tell which character is speaking.
- "Judith is so mean!!!"
- Dialogue is a little long for early game but solid dialogue in terms of quality.
	- Would be nice to discover more characters later.
	- "I don't think you should change her personality, I like that she's mean." -- Zada.
	- Suggestion: Maybe shorten her first meeting and focus on her telling you to do the puzzle.
	- Spread out the lore over a few puzzles to help cut down the bulk.
	- There is a lot of story that they're being hit with at once.

B. Puzzle Mode
- "Ohhh we select the tile!" Nicole said.
	- Wasn't sure what to do upon first entry into puzzle mode.
	- Began to understand through the yellow highlight feedback on the selected tile.
- Emphasized the need for a tutorial, perhaps little pop ups.
	- i.e., not a full tutorial, just something to get users started.
- Confused about the how ice tiles work.
- Likes the multiple options to solve puzzle.
- Couldn't restart the second puzzle.
- Mana points deducted even when a tile was blocked from moving.
- It appears like Skye is not visible on the tiles in the second puzzle.
	- Either she's just very hard to see or she's not being configured correctly in Puzzle2.prefab. Need to check this.
- Check that the backend data (e.g., move count values) are correct on Puzzle2.prefab.
	- Is Puzzle 2 even solvable?
- After I quickly explained the ice mechanic, Nicole understood it well enough to explain it to Ayoka.
	- Easy to learn mechanic!
- Ayoka was confused about the numbers on the puzzle cards.
	- She actually thought she had to click a number on the keyboard that cooresponded to a card.
- Nicole: With the energy limit being higher than the moves available, this makes the puzzles feel a bit redundant.
	- Can't do anything with that extra Mana.
	- Would be better to have more Move Cards than Mana available because that lends itself to more possibilities.
	- Gives an opportunity for greater flexibility for those early puzzles.
	- This could be a backend data error in terms of the resource system, should have the engineers cross reference it with the Figma data.

C. Physical & Visual Navigation
- Nicole walked straight into the void.
	- Didn't even see the first puzzle terminal and there were no instructions on how to enter the puzzle.
	- Suggestion: Waypoint possibly pointing you to the location of the first puzzle.
- Found the terminal for the second puzzle very easily after completing the first one.
- Cannot see normal tiles on the white backdrop of the snow.
- Suggestion: Add directional arrows onto cards to indicate the direction that the tiles can move in.
	- Both Nicole and Zada have a hard time telling left from right, so this would be a helpful accessibility feature for them.
- Need to make Skye's icon more visible in puzzle mode.
- Need to ensure she is locked on the right axis so she's not turning.
- Difficulty indicators, possibly?
	- i.e., easy puzzle, harder puzzle.
	- Might not apply as much if all puzzles need to be solved in the same order.
- Navigating the overworld, would be nice if the angle followed the character a little more smoothly.
